Welcome to my dinosaur scratch page. This section introduces quick facts about prehistoric life while demonstrating styled text and layout blocks for my assignment.

Fun Fact 1: Dinosaurs are a group of reptiles that have lived on Earth for about 245 million years.

Fun Fact 2: In 1842, the English naturalist Sir Richard Owen coined the term Dinosauria, derived from the Greek deinos, meaning “fearfully great,” and sauros, meaning “lizard.”.

Fun Fact 3: There are roughly 700 known species of extinct dinosaurs.

Why These Facts Matter

New fossils and methods continue to reshape dinosaur science. Comparing size, habitat, and anatomy helps explain how different species adapted and survived in changing ecosystems.
