Spinosaurus Fun Facts

Dinosaur Fun Fact Generator

Press the button to generate a dinosaur fact.

Largest Known Carnivore

Estimated around 46 to 59 feet long, making it longer than the average Tyrannosaurus rex.

Built for Water

Its body proportions suggest it spent significant time in rivers and coastal habitats, unlike many other giant theropods.

Iconic Sail

The tall neural spines on its back formed a huge sail that may have helped with display, species recognition, or heat exchange.

Movie Star

Spinosaurus became a pop culture favorite after its big-screen role in Jurassic Park III.
