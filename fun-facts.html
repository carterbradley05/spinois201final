Featured Fun Facts (Numbered + Bulleted)

  1. Largest Known Carnivore
    • Estimated around 46 to 59 feet long, often longer than average Tyrannosaurus rex estimates.
  2. Built for Water
    • Its body proportions suggest heavy use of rivers and coastal habitats compared to many giant theropods.
  3. Iconic Sail
    • Tall neural spines formed a sail likely used for display, thermoregulation, or species recognition.
  4. Movie Star
    • Spinosaurus became a major pop-culture dinosaur after its role in Jurassic Park III.
