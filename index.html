A Giant

As mentioned above, the Spinosaurus is the largest known carnivore, and is estimated to have been around 46-59 feet long. This is much larger than the t-rex, which was around 40 feet long. Some scientists believe it weighed up 20,000 kg, which means that only 100 would be needed to weigh as much as a space shuttle.

Award Winning Actor

The Spinosaurus was featured as the main predator in blockbuster hit Jurrassic Park III (2001). The movie went on to be nominated for several awards.

Semi-Aquatic Sail

The Spinosaurus recieved its name from its distinguishible sail that extends along its back. Scientists are not sure what the sail was used for, but some believe it may have been used for thermoregulation, while others believe it may have been used for display purposes. The Spinosaurus is also believed to have been semi-aquatic, and may have used its sail to help it swim.
